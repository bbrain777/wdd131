ZION Youth Development Initiative - Website Plan

Empowering young people for a brighter future

Site Name

ZION Youth Development Initiative

This name reflects the core mission of the NGO to support and empower youth through meaningful programs. The name ‘ZION’ symbolizes strength, unity, and purpose.

Optional domain: zionyouth.org

Site Purpose

The website will serve as an official digital presence for the ZION Youth Development Initiative. It will:

  • Inform visitors about our mission and programs
  • Provide opportunities for volunteering and skill development
  • Enable visitors to sign up for programs or contact us
  • Encourage donations and community engagement

Scenarios

  • How can I register for one of the upcoming youth development programs?
  • Where can I donate to support ZION’s mission?

Color Schema

  • Forest Green (#2a7f62): Used for headings, buttons, and the header background.
  • Warm Orange (#f4a261): Used for accents like borders, links, and call-to-action highlights.
  • Light Grey Background (#f9f9f9): Used as the primary page background for readability.
